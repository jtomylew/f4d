About Cheyenne

Generally I eat to survive, but there’s something about cooking and eating with people I love. It’s so nice to slow down and gather around food as an excuse for connecting with others. There was one time where we built these epic tent forts in my friend’s backyard eating food and listening to other people read poems...beautiful.
